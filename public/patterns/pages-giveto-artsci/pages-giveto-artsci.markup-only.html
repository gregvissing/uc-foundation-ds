Arts & Sciences

By supporting the UC Fund for Arts and Sciences, you are joining a team of alumni and donors committed to making a meaningful impact in our students' lives and thereby the world. It is only through your collective support that we can create the next breakthrough at UC's College of Arts and Sciences.

Your Investment Creates...

Funding for research that encourages interconnectedness with our campus and our global community.

Graduate student support which allows students to focus on their research and education by alleviating the financial burden of research costs, conference attendance, professional development and more.

Opportunity for us to address our greatest needs with unrestricted funds, enabling the college to support the areas that need it most.

Innovative programs like our new School of Environment and Sustainability to help share the importance of a sustainable world.
